# FlowZen — First Impression Experience Specification

## 1. First Launch Experience

### 0–3 Seconds: The Soft Awake
- **App Opening**: A tranquil, warm-off-white canvas (`#FBF9F5`) smoothly expands from the center of the screen.
- **Logo Animation**: In the screen center, an elegant golden-leafed lotus emblem subtly breathes with a gentle 2.5-second radial glow pulse.
- **Sound**: A warm, resonant bamboo chime chord combined with a soft, natural acoustic wind swell plays at 20% volume.
- **Background Visuals**: Ultra-subtle morning light particles drift slowly upwards across the canvas, setting a serene tone.

### 3–10 Seconds: Emotional Introduction
- **Visual Shift**: The lotus logo gently transitions upward into the top navigation header while a quiet, poetic headline fades in: *"Welcome to your living digital sanctuary."*
- **Ambient Audio**: Gentle mountain stream trickles and rustling bamboo foliage audio layer in at low volume.
- **Emotional Impact**: Physical shoulder tension drops; the user experiences immediate curiosity, visual warmth, and parasympathetic calm.

### 10–30 Seconds: World Discovery
- **Visual Shift**: A real-time preview of the user's initial garden courtyard unfurls in a central hero frame. Soft morning mist rolls across a mossy stone plot.
- **Interactive Tease**: A subtle pulsing light highlight surrounds a small bamboo shoot, inviting the user's first touch.
- **Narrative Framing**: A warm, brief message appears: *"Every step you take in movement breathes life into your sanctuary."*

### 30–60 Seconds: First Interaction
- **User Action**: The user taps the pulsing bamboo shoot or the prominent *"Begin Your Sanctuary"* button.
- **Visual Response**: The bamboo shoot instantly sprouts a vibrant green leaf as expanding concentric ring ripples radiate across the garden soil with a soft wooden chime sound.
- **Smooth Transition**: The interface seamlessly transitions into Step 1 of the gentle onboarding sequence.

---

## 2. Splash Screen Design

- **Visual Concept**: Minimalist, high-contrast, warm-sand canvas featuring an organic, hand-crafted golden lotus emblem framed by spacious negative space.
- **Animation**: 2.5-second breathing pulse cycle (`opacity` and `scale` ease-in-out from 0.95 to 1.05) with floating ambient light micro-particles.
- **Sound**: Soft harmonic bamboo chime tuned to 432Hz combined with ambient wind recording.
- **Duration**: Exactly 2.5 seconds total before smoothly fading into the onboarding introduction.
- **Emotional Goal**: To instantly shift the user from a state of digital busyness and distraction into a state of present, tranquil awareness.

---

## 3. Onboarding Journey

### Step 1: User Identity
- **Screen Purpose**: Introduce the user to their personalized journey and collect their preferred name or title.
- **User Emotion**: Respected, welcomed, and personally acknowledged.
- **Information Collected**: Practitioner name or preferred title (e.g., *"Master Ebram"* or *"Ebram"*).

### Step 2: Wellness Goals
- **Screen Purpose**: Understand the user's primary intentions for physical movement and mindfulness.
- **User Emotion**: Understood, supported, and motivated by clear personal intent.
- **Information Collected**: Primary focus areas (e.g., *Posture & Spine Alignment*, *Stress Relief & Anxiety*, *Energy & Vitality*, *Mindful Movement & Balance*).

### Step 3: Experience Preference
- **Screen Purpose**: Gauge the user's familiarity with Tai Chi, Qigong, and diaphragmatic breathing to tailor practice pacing.
- **User Emotion**: Reassured that no prior experience or physical flexibility is required.
- **Information Collected**: Somatic experience level (*Beginner*, *Intermediate*, *Advanced*) and preferred daily session duration (*5 mins*, *10 mins*, *15 mins*).

### Step 4: Personal Sanctuary Creation
- **Screen Purpose**: Allow the user to select their initial elemental garden alignment to personalize their starting sanctuary.
- **User Emotion**: Deep emotional ownership, excitement, and creative agency.
- **Information Collected**: Initial Elemental Alignment choice (*Air — Clarity & Breath*, *Water — Flow & Mobility*, *Fire — Energy & Focus*, *Earth — Grounding & Stability*).

### Step 5: First Practice Invitation
- **Screen Purpose**: Present today's custom-tailored 3-minute introductory movement form with a frictionless single-tap start button.
- **User Emotion**: Ready, curious, and empowered to begin their first physical session.
- **Information Collected**: User confirmation to launch the first practice session.

---

## 4. First Session Experience

- **How the User Starts**: Tapping *"Begin First Session"* smoothly dissolves the onboarding UI into a full-screen, tranquil practice player canvas. Ambient nature soundscapes swell smoothly as a 2D avatar skeleton initializes in a grounded stance.
- **How AI Introduces Itself**: The Somatic AI Coach speaks with a warm, calm, and reassuring voice: *"Welcome, practitioner. Stand comfortably, drop your shoulders, and feel the earth beneath your feet. Today, we practice the foundation of flow."*
- **How Progress is Rewarded**: During the 3-minute session, real-time posture feedback validates effort with soft glowing aura rings around avatar joints and gentle audio chime confirmations. Upon completion, a golden particle burst shower celebrates the milestone.
- **How Sanctuary Changes Afterward**: Returning from the session, the user witnesses their initial courtyard transform in real-time: fresh emerald grass sprouts, a trickling stream begins flowing, and their first stone lantern lights automatically with a warm sunset glow.

---

## 5. Conversion Psychology

FlowZen introduces premium value ethically through moments of genuine delight and appreciation, never through high-stress paywalls or urgent popups:

1. **The Post-Session Clarity Moment**: After completing a deeply restorative practice, the user is offered a quiet preview of advanced AI posture depth and specialized audio soundscapes without interrupting their post-practice calm.
2. **The Garden Evolution Preview**: When customizing their living sanctuary, locked legendary items (e.g., *Celestial Pagoda*, *Golden Fox Companion*) can be freely previewed in real-time, allowing users to appreciate their aesthetic beauty before deciding to subscribe.
3. **The Mastery Milestone Celebration**: Reaching a major rank milestone (e.g., *Disciple*) triggers a celebratory summary that highlights the extra features included in the *FlowZen Pass* as a natural, optional next step in their lifelong journey.

---

## 6. Implementation Priority

For maximum user retention and immediate product impact, the first-impression elements should be implemented in this priority order:

1. **High-Impact Splash & Launch Audio**: Establish the 2.5s lotus animation and bamboo chime chord for immediate sensory calm.
2. **Frictionless 5-Step Onboarding Sequence**: Build the clean, warm-sand onboarding slides with preference collection and elemental garden selection.
3. **Interactive Sanctuary Transformation**: Connect the first session completion trigger directly to the real-time garden sprouting animation (Stage 1 to Stage 2).
4. **First 3-Minute Practice Player Flow**: Deliver a pristine, high-contrast practice player view with warm AI coach voice prompts and skeleton pose guidance.
5. **Post-Practice Celebration & Reward Screen**: Build the golden particle completion animation and Qi XP reward summary.
