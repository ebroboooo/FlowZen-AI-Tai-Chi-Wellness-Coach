# FlowZen — Gamification & Retention Specification

## 1. Retention Philosophy
Retention in FlowZen is anchored in intrinsic peace, positive habit cultivation, and emotional ownership of a living digital sanctuary. Rather than relying on stressful countdown timers or aggressive punishment mechanics, FlowZen inspires daily returns through visual transformation, tranquil consistency, and restorative daily mindfulness routines. Users return because their sanctuary is a peaceful, living reflection of their physical and mental dedication.

---

## 2. Progression System

### XP (Somatic Experience)
- Earned through completed Tai Chi forms, Qigong sessions, breathing exercises, and posture feedback accuracy.
- Scales gracefully with practice duration and mindfulness consistency to advance the practitioner's overall level.

### Levels
- Numerical progression tier (Levels 1 to 100) unlocking new sanctuary placement slots, decorative features, audio soundscape layers, and custom avatar stances.

### Mastery
- 5 somatic progression ranks representing physical form mastery:
  1. **Student**: Fundamental stance and breath alignment.
  2. **Disciple**: Smooth movement transitions and elemental rhythm.
  3. **Practitioner**: Biomechanical accuracy and posture stability.
  4. **Master**: Effortless flow, deep diaphragmatic harmony, and form precision.
  5. **Grand Master**: Complete elemental balance across Air, Water, Fire, and Earth.

### Daily Energy (Qi)
- A non-punitive daily vitality metric recharged naturally each day or through morning mindfulness sessions.
- Spent to nurture garden flora, feed koi fish, or initiate specialized elemental cultivation sessions.

### Zen Coins (Soft Currency)
- Earned purely through practice completion, daily streaks, quest achievements, and garden care.
- Used in the Sanctuary Shop to acquire decorative items, ambient music tracks, seasonal weather filters, and garden wildlife habitats.

---

## 3. Daily Rewards

### 30-Day Login Cycle
A recurring monthly calendar offering increasing rewards for consistent check-ins:
- **Days 1–6**: Daily Zen Coins, Qi Energy refills, and garden soil nutrients.
- **Day 7 (Weekly Milestone)**: Unlocks an exclusive decorative item (e.g., Stone Lantern / Cherry Blossom Sapling).
- **Days 8–13**: Increased Zen Coins, rare flower seeds, and elemental soundscape snippets.
- **Day 14 (Mid-Month Milestone)**: Rare wildlife habitat (e.g., Koi Fish Pond feature).
- **Days 15–27**: Tiered currency, weather filters, and special animation stances.
- **Day 28–30 (Grand Milestone)**: Unique seasonal sanctuary architecture or rare animal companion (e.g., Red Panda / Golden Fox).

### Daily Quests
3 simple, achievable daily goals that reward mindful practice:
1. *Morning Flow*: Complete 1 Tai Chi form before noon (Reward: 50 Zen Coins + 20 XP).
2. *Breath Harmony*: Practice 5 minutes of elemental diaphragmatic breathing (Reward: 40 Zen Coins + 15 XP).
3. *Sanctuary Nurture*: Water garden plants or interact with garden wildlife (Reward: 30 Zen Coins).

### Weekly Quests
Broader goals rewarding consistency over 7-day cycles:
- Complete 5 practice sessions across the week.
- Practice forms across 2 different elemental paths (e.g., Air and Earth).
- Maintain a 5-day practice streak.

### Monthly Challenges
Theme-driven monthly somatic journeys (e.g., *Month of the Flowing River*, *Spring Renewal Journey*) rewarding exclusive seasonal titles, rare garden flora, and custom sky themes.

## 4. Sanctuary Progression (12 Stages)

1. **Stage 1: Humbled Courtyard** — A simple earthen plot with a single mossy boulder and morning mist.
2. **Stage 2: Sprouting Clearing** — Young grass patches and small bamboo shoots emerge around a wooden bench.
3. **Stage 3: Stone & Stream** — A gentle winding stone path and a clear trickling freshwater stream appear.
4. **Stage 4: Lantern Glade** — Hand-carved granite stone lanterns illuminate the pathway during twilight.
5. **Stage 5: Lotus Pavilion** — A serene wooden pavilion with a small lily pond and flowering water blossoms.
6. **Stage 6: Bamboo Grove** — Towering emerald bamboo stands creating rustling ambient wind acoustics.
7. **Stage 7: Bridge of Reflection** — An arched red wooden bridge spanning over an expanded koi fish pond.
8. **Stage 8: Blossom Orchard** — Flowering Japanese cherry blossom (Sakura) trees with drifting petal particle effects.
9. **Stage 9: Waterfall Sanctuary** — A tiered stone waterfall flowing into the sanctuary stream with cascading water mist.
10. **Stage 10: Celestial Pagoda** — An elegant multi-tiered Zen pagoda framed by mountain ridges and sunset light.
11. **Stage 11: Mist Mountain Vista** — Elevated cloud banks, flying crane silhouettes, and ancient pine trees.
12. **Stage 12: Legendary Zen Realm** — A breath-taking, fully customizable heavenly sanctuary featuring golden light, dragon-stone arches, aurora skies, and wildlife habitats.

## 6. Achievement System
FlowZen achievements reward dedication, mindfulness consistency, and personal growth rather than grinding:

- *First Step*: Complete your first Tai Chi form.
- *7-Day Tranquility*: Maintain a 7-day practice streak.
- *30-Day Mindfulness*: Complete a practice session every day for 30 consecutive days.
- *Elemental Balance*: Reach level 50 in all 4 Elemental Paths (Air, Water, Fire, Earth).
- *Sanctuary Architect*: Place 25 unique decorative items in your living garden.
- *Form Devotee*: Practice 100 total hours of somatic movement.
- *Inner Calm*: Complete 50 diaphragmatic breathing sessions.
- *Harmony Master*: Unlock all 12 Sanctuary Progression Stages.

## 8. Monetization Philosophy

### Allowed
- **Premium Subscription (FlowZen Pass)**: Grants access to exclusive seasonal garden cosmetics, advanced AI coach depth, unlimited high-tier audio soundscapes, and cloud backup.
- **Direct Cosmetic Purchases**: Optional purchase of aesthetic sanctuary decorations, unique sky themes, and rare animal companions.
- **Optional Rewarded Ads**: Non-intrusive, strictly user-initiated video views for bonus Zen Coins (e.g., "Watch a peaceful showcase to earn 20 extra Zen Coins").

### Never Allowed
- **Pay-To-Win**: Core Tai Chi curriculum, posture accuracy tracking, and basic mastery progress are always 100% free for all users.
- **Forced Ads**: Never show popups, interstitial banner ads, or unskippable auto-play clips.
- **Interrupting Meditation**: No commercial messaging or notifications will ever trigger during or immediately around practice or sanctuary immersion.
