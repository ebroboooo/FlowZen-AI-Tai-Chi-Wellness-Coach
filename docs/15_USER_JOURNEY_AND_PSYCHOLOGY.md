# FlowZen — User Journey & Psychological Design Specification

## 1. Core Psychological Principles

FlowZen’s engagement model is built on parasympathetic design and positive human psychology rather than stress-inducing urgency loops:

- **Intrinsic Motivation**: Practice is driven by internal peace, physical vitality, and body awareness rather than external social pressure or artificial deadlines.
- **Calm Progression**: Advancement occurs smoothly and naturally. Milestones reflect genuine physical alignment and mindful dedication without frantic grinding.
- **Curiosity**: The living garden sanctuary is an evolving world filled with gentle discovery, subtle wildlife behaviors, and changing atmospheric light.
- **Emotional Ownership**: As practitioners invest time into daily movement, their garden reflects their personal journey, instilling deep pride and care for their digital sanctuary.
- **Habit Formation**: Built through rewarding micro-routines, gentle morning triggers, and frictionless single-tap practice access that integrates effortlessly into daily life.
- **Reward Anticipation**: Delights practitioners with peaceful surprises upon opening the app—blooming water lilies, visiting wildlife, and warm morning light.
- **Self-Reflection**: Encourages daily somatic awareness, mood check-ins, and posture reflection guided by a supportive AI master.

## 2. User Journey Timeline

### First 30 Seconds
- **User Action**: Opens FlowZen for the first time.
- **Psychological Experience**: Immediate sensory calm. Soft bamboo chime tones play as warm morning mist drifts across a simple earthen courtyard. Shoulders lower, breathing slows, and anxiety dissipates.
- **Emotional State**: Serene curiosity and visual delight.

### First Session (Minutes 1–10)
- **User Action**: Follows a gentle 5-minute *Wave Hands Like Clouds* introductory form with 2D avatar guidance.
- **Psychological Experience**: Effortless physical movement paired with responsive elemental audio. Posture feedback is gentle and encouraging.
- **Emotional State**: Competence, empowerment, and physical grounding.

### First Day
- **User Action**: Returns in the evening to inspect their sanctuary and claim their first daily practice reward.
- **Psychological Experience**: Observes twilight setting over the courtyard as a young pine tree sprouts where they practiced earlier.
- **Emotional State**: Anticipation and quiet satisfaction.

### First Week
- **User Action**: Completes 5 short daily sessions, exploring Air and Water elemental paths.
- **Psychological Experience**: The sanctuary transforms from a bare plot into a trickling stream with stone lanterns. Posture awareness begins bleeding into off-screen daily life.
- **Emotional State**: Habitual momentum, emotional attachment to the garden.

### First Month
- **User Action**: Achieves the rank of *Disciple*, unlocks the Koi Pond feature, and converses regularly with the Somatic AI Coach.
- **Psychological Experience**: Practice feels as natural as morning tea or coffee. The user feels physically stronger, more flexible, and noticeably calmer under stress.
- **Emotional State**: Deep pride, identity shift ("I am a practitioner").

### First 3 Months
- **User Action**: Explores advanced Tai Chi forms, customized garden weather settings, and seasonal events.
- **Psychological Experience**: The sanctuary is now a lush, personalized oasis filled with bamboo groves, flowering cherry trees, and visiting wildlife.
- **Emotional State**: Deep emotional ownership and daily sanctuary refuge.

### First Year
- **User Action**: Reaches *Master* status, looking back over 365 days of movement history and garden evolution.
- **Psychological Experience**: FlowZen has become an indispensable life companion—a tranquil anchor for physical vitality and mental clarity.
- **Emotional State**: Transformation, gratitude, and lifelong commitment to physical mindfulness.

## 4. AI Master Personality

### Personality Traits
- **Somatic Mentor**: Wise, compassionate, observant, patient, and grounded.
- **Deeply Respectful**: Treats the user as an equal partner in their personal cultivation journey.
- **Calm & Present**: Never rushed, hyperactive, or overly enthusiastic. Speaks with poise and quiet certainty.

### Speaking Style & Vocabulary
- **Style**: Concise, poetic, clear, and reassuring. Uses metaphors drawn from nature, water, wind, and trees.
- **Preferred Vocabulary**: *Flow, grounding, alignment, vitality, sanctuary, breath, root, harmony, patience, presence, balance*.

### Things the AI Should NEVER Say
- ❌ *"You failed your streak!"* or *"Where have you been?!"*
- ❌ *"You need to work harder!"* or *"No pain, no gain!"*
- ❌ *"Hurry up and complete your daily goals!"*
- ❌ *"You lost your rewards!"*

### How It Celebrates Progress
- Soft, meaningful validation highlighting internal physical growth:
  - *"Notice how your stance anchored with greater ease today. Your roots grow deeper with every breath."*
  - *"Your movement flowed like water through the transition. Honor the stillness you created within."*

### How It Responds After Missed Days
- Warm, non-judgmental welcome emphasizing unconditional presence:
  - *"Welcome back, friend. The river does not rush, nor does it regret where it rested. Whenever you step onto the path, your sanctuary remains open."*
  - *"Life has its seasons of rest. Your garden waited patiently, and your roots remain strong."*

## 5. Notification Philosophy

### Core Rules
1. **Never Guilt-Trip**: Never use negative reinforcement, loss-aversion threats, or guilt mechanics.
2. **Never Pressure**: Avoid urgent deadlines, exclamation points, or aggressive call-to-actions.
3. **Always Inspire**: Focus on peace, physical relief, beauty, and emotional warmth.
